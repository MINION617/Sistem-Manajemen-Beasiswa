# Feature Research

## Table Stakes

### Student Portal

- Secure login for mahasiswa.
- Profile and academic data management.
- Scholarship discovery and online application submission.
- Status tracking for application progress and disbursement.

### Admin Operations

- CRUD sponsor and scholarship program management.
- Applicant document review and verification decisions.
- Complaint queue handling and resolution updates.

### Selection Workflow

- Test and interview score entry.
- Final recipient assignment after plenary decision.
- Decision support views for leadership.

### Finance and Oversight

- Transfer proof upload and payout status updates.
- Recipient list visibility by scholarship and sponsor.
- Financial dashboard and sponsor-based fund allocation reports.
- Verification of transfer proof for audit.

## Differentiators Present In The Brief

- Realtime notifications for status changes and fund transfers.
- Complaint lifecycle visibility for both students and management.
- Dedicated executive views for Kabag Kemahasiswaan and Wakil Bagian Keuangan.

## Likely Supporting Capabilities

- Role-based dashboards.
- Search, filter, and drill-down across applicants and scholarships.
- Historical status timeline for each application.
- Export-friendly reporting structures for sponsor and finance review.
